You show phase completion but not phase starts. Add toasts for:

Phase 1 (ISRC recovery) starting
Phase 3 (MusicBrainz) starting
Phase 4 (Chartmetric) starting
Phase 5 (MLC) starting
Why it matters: Users can see the pipeline progress in real-time, not just completions.

4. Bulk Operation Progress
When users bulk enrich 50+ tracks, they get one toast at the start. Add:

Progress toast that updates every 10 tracks
Completion summary with success/fail counts
Error details for failed tracks
5. Playlist Metadata Updates
You broadcast playlist_updated but don't show toasts. Add notification when:

Playlist follower count increases significantly (>10%)
Playlist curator changes
Playlist becomes unavailable/deleted
6. Live Activity Feed (Lower Priority)
Currently activity logs aren't broadcast. Consider adding:

Small notification badge on sidebar when new activity occurs
Optional live activity feed panel
